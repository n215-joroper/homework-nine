Why Nintendo?

When it comes to original games, nobody does it better than Nintendo. With amazing titles in series like The Legend of Zelda, Pokémon, and Animal Crossing, Nintendo is constantly creating the most popular games on the market. While other games can be played on multiple consoles or PC, you can only play these games on a Nintendo Switch.

Nintendo's commitment to the Switch is evident in its Nintendo Switch Online service. For most paid games, you'll need a Nintendo Switch Online subscription to play online. However, the subscriptions are cheaper compared to other consoles. For example, an online pass is just $20 annually, compared to $60 for Xbox and PlayStation.

Gaming is an experience that should be shared with friends, and the Nintendo Switch is the best console on the market for this. Thanks to the detachable Joy-Con, you don't have to worry about buying an extra controller to play with a friend.

Nintendo's mission is to put smiles on the faces of everyone we touch. We do so by creating new surprises for people across the world to enjoy together. We've forged our own path since 1889, when we began making hanafuda playing cards in Kyoto, Japan. Today, we’re fortunate to be able to share our characters, ideas and worlds through the medium of video games and the entertainment industry.

Nintendo of America, established in 1980 and based in Redmond, Wash., is a wholly owned subsidiary of Nintendo Co., Ltd. We are committed to delivering best-in-class products and services to our customers and to investing in the well-being of our employees as part of the global Nintendo family.
